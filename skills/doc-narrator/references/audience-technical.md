# Audience: Technical

Default audience. Architects, engineers, and tech writers who will read — and contribute to — the document.

## Writing defaults

- Prose before diagrams. Diagram confirms what was just said.
- Cold-reader test: a reader unfamiliar with the project should follow the document without external context.
- Domain terms are linked on first use to the project glossary (`[term](glossary#term)`). Subsequent uses omit the link.
- Diagram types: Mermaid or D2 sequence, ER, and C4 Level diagrams.
- Deep technical detail belongs in `<details>` collapsibles, not inline.
- Open questions are first-class. Use a natural unresolved-decisions header with numbered items.
- Plain language always. Prefer the verb over its nominalized noun. Cut adjectives that don't discriminate ("advanced", "intelligent", "sophisticated"). One idea per sentence. See writing-patterns.md § Plain Language.
